# Stocks-gsb

Stocks-gsb is an online webb application to make it easier to overview the pricing of gold, silver and bronze in different currencies. 

## Description

With Stocks-gsb it's possible to see live changes of the stock market of these three metals as well as the ability to buy and sell them whenever you want. Several news reports and telegrams will be att your disposal, as well as chatbots, to guide you throuch the whole process, help you out on the webb site and give recommendation on what to do nexts.
